Welcome to Ganesh Corporation

Ganesh Corporation is one of the leading traders in the scrap recycling industry for over 15 years.

Our founder Kumaresan Rangaswamy believes in growing with responsibility and quality excellence while developing long term business relationships based on reliability and transparency.

We aim to be the best in what we do and pride ourselves in being completely transparent while offering the highest quality of services in recycling scrap metal - stainless steel, aluminium, brass, copper insulated wire and other scrap metals in any condition.

Buying, selling, recycling scrap metal is done under strict quality guidelines and we are committed to operate our facility in a manner that protects health and the environment.

Ganesh Corporation has extended its recycling operations globally. Our growth and success is built on highest standards of service making us the preferred choice for collaboration in managing scrap, by clients from around the world.

Do not hesitate to contact us for a hassle free service and for advice regarding disposal of any scrap.
